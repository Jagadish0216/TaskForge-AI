# TaskForge AI — Brand Logo Concept & Visual Mark Specifications

This document defines the symbol geometry, logo construction, safe margins, dark/light variants, and app icon standards for TaskForge AI.

---

## 1. Symbol Inspiration & Geometry
The TaskForge AI emblem synthesizes three core architectural concepts into a single geometric mark:

```
      ╔═══════════════════╗
      ║     ┌───────┐     ║   1. Stacked Layers (Software Architecture & Sprint Pipelines)
      ║    ┌┴──────┐│     ║   2. Anvil / Forge Symbol (Craftsmanship & Creation)
      ║   ┌┴───────┤│     ║   3. Interlocking Nodes (Team Collaboration & AI Nodes)
      ║   └────────┘┘     ║
      ╚═══════════════════╝
```

- **Primary Mark**: Three overlapping translucent rounded layers angled upwards to represent continuous delivery and progress momentum.
- **Sparkle Star Accent**: A four-point vector star positioned at the upper-right vertex representing intelligence and automated optimization.

---

## 2. Logo Mark Variants

### A. Full Brandmark (Primary Horizontal)
Used on Landing Page header, Navbar, marketing decks, and enterprise documentation.
- **Icon**: Gradient square container (`from-blue-600 to-indigo-600`) housing white `Layers` mark.
- **Logotype**: Bold inter-styled sans typography `"TaskForge AI"`.
- **Version Sub-badge**: Micro pill badge `v2.5` in semi-transparent blue.

### B. Icon Mark (Square App Icon / Favicon)
Used for browser favicons, desktop shortcuts, mobile app icons, and social avatars.
- **Background**: `bg-slate-950` or `bg-slate-900` with 1px border.
- **Core Vector**: Gradient `Layers` emblem centered with 25% corner radius.

---

## 3. Safe Spacing & Clear Zone Rules
To ensure optical balance and visual prominence, the brand mark must always retain a clear buffer zone free from competing visual elements or body text.

- **Clear Zone Rule**: `1.5x` where `x` equals the height of the emblem mark.
- **Minimum Render Size**:
  - Web Nav Header: `24px x 24px` (Emblem) + `14px` (Logotype).
  - Favicon / App Icon: `16px x 16px` minimum up to `512px x 512px`.

---

## 4. Light vs. Dark Mode Adaptations

| Surface Mode | Emblem Background | Logotype Text Color | AI Accent Sparkle |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dark Theme (Default)** | Gradient Blue-to-Indigo | Pure White (`#FFFFFF`) | Cyan/Blue 400 (`#60A5FA`) |
| **Light Theme** | Gradient Blue-to-Indigo | Slate 900 (`#0F172A`) | Indigo 600 (`#4F46E5`) |
| **Monochrome Print** | Solid Black or White | Solid Black (`#000000`) | Solid Accent |

---

## 5. Unacceptable Usage (Do's & Don'ts)
- ❌ **DO NOT** skew, stretch, or rotate the emblem angle.
- ❌ **DO NOT** change the gradient colors to unapproved palette choices (e.g., bright pink or neon yellow).
- ❌ **DO NOT** drop heavy black drop shadows directly under the logotype text.
- ❌ **DO NOT** place the dark logotype variant on dark navy backgrounds.
